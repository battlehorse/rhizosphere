Use with iGoogle

This page describes how you can use Rhizosphere gadgets inside your iGoogle homepage.

To use Rhizosphere in your iGoogle page, first you identify a Google Spreadsheet that contains the data you want to display. Once you have it, follow these instructions to setup the gadget inside the spreadsheet.

From within the spreadsheet, open the gadget menu from the top-right corner of the Rhizosphere Gadget, and choose the Add Gadget to iGoogle option. That's it. Really easy (thanks Google)!

Tip. By clicking the maximize icon in the top-right corner of the Rhizosphere gadget, you can switch to fullscreen mode, exactly in the same way as described for the spreadsheet case.
